What is Paradox?

Pardox Interactive is a Swedish game development studio best known for historical grand strategy games. They are based in Stockholm, hoowever, their games are popular internationally. They carry a mostly positive reputation, creating quality video games and continuing to support games years after launch. This continued support for titles they've released makes consumers happy, having a game perfected rather than a new title coming out every year. Many of these titles are supported by their most well known subsidiary, Paradox Development Studio.
