About

I have played all of these game series' latest additions and have enjoyed each one. Europa Universalis is the most balanced in the gameplay, you play as a country and control it for the game's time period. Crusader Kings takes a different approach on mechanics, having the player play as a dynasty instead of as a country. Hearts of Iron focuses on the war aspect of a country, and Victoria focuses on economics primarily.
