Formating


Why is formating important?

Formating is important because it creates a standard across all of research papers. It allows you to oragnize your research in way that is unifrom and easy for your teacher to understand. There are many different aspects of formatting, following your teacher's guidlines are the best method for going about this


Areas of Formating

Endnotes

Endnotes are where you put your citations that you used in your paper. Any quote, paraphrase, or summary must be cited in this portion of your paper. You must list the full source the first time you cite it in your endnotes, after that you can use an abbreviated version.

Bibliography

The bibliography is a list of all sources used in alphabetical order. You can do a biblography manually, and there are many guides for this. However, an easier way is to use bibliogrpahy generators such as EasyBib. They will make you life a million times easier.
